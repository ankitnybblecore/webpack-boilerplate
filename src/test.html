About us

Maze began with the belief that product design teams should have easy access to data during the design phase. Since then we have evolved our vision to empower anyone in product and marketing teams to test, learn, and act rapidly. We believe that this is how you build successful user experiences today.

Building user-facing experiences is always a risk. And the riskier the decision, the more data we need to fully commit. It’s not easy, however, to validate ideas and get actionable, quantifiable user insights to make decisions confidently, especially before you have a live product or website.
